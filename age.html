Conclusions

Looking at these charts, it's pretty clear that people in their late twenties through early sixties are facing both fines and charges, with the 26-39 group getting hit the hardest with over 20,000 fines and nearly 100 charges. What's really striking though is just how few charges there are compared to fines - we're talking about over 52,000 fines versus only 229 charges total, which means most people are just getting slapped with a fine rather than facing serious legal trouble. The younger and older folks seem to be flying under the radar for the most part, especially kids under 16 and seniors over 65.
