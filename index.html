About Us

The Traffic Violations Data Center is a public initiative dedicated to enhancing road safety and accountability through transparent data analytics. Our platform compiles and visualizes comprehensive datasets related to traffic violations, with a primary focus on speeding incidents.

Our mission is to empower policymakers, researchers, journalists, and concerned citizens with reliable insights into traffic law enforcement. We aim to promote data-driven decisions that contribute to safer roads and more equitable traffic law practices.

This project is developed by a cross-functional team of data analysts, software developers, and civic technologists who believe in open access to public information. All data used on this platform is sourced from state and federal transportation agencies and is updated regularly to ensure accuracy and relevance.

If you have feedback, suggestions, or data contributions, feel free to contact us.
